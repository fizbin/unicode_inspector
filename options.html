The following replacement text is available:

  • {cp} - the codepoint in four to six digits of hexadecimal (this is probably what you want)
  • {cpShort} - the codepoint in hexadecimal
  • {cpDecimal} - the codepoint in decimal
  • {utf8} - the codepoint in percent-encoded utf8
  • {cp.u} - the same value as {cp}, but upper case
  • {cpShort.u} - the same value as {cpShort}, but upper case
